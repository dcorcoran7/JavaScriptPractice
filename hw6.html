General Membership

Every student who sits in the student section on game days is considered to be a general member of Cassell Guard.

Executive Board

The Executive Board consists of die-hard Virginia Tech Basketball fans that are hand-selected each year through an application process.

Executive Board Responsibilities:

  1. Organize giveaways for students
  2. Run the Cassell Guard social media account
  3. Plan events to boost fan interaction
